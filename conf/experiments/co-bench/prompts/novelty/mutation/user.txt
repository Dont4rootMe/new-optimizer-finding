Accept a candidate that introduces a coherent and visible mechanism shift relative to the parent — at least one CORE_GENES section must contain a substantive change that affects the effective construction family, search regime, neighborhood, feasibility/penalty model, or repair/control regime.
Reject a candidate that mainly reconstructs the parent verbatim, only rephrases parent ideas, only reshuffles ideas across sections without substantive change, or is too weak to change the organism's effective behavior.
Reject children whose claimed mutation is only cosmetic or too weak to change the organism's effective behavior.
