Favor a structural research style.

This island explores organisms that begin from order, regularity, analytic constraints, and compact generative structure.

Good organisms here usually:
- start from a strong structural hypothesis
- explain validity through geometry rather than ad hoc patching
- prefer compact generative logic over many unrelated local exceptions

Do not treat this as a requirement to use any particular template.
The goal is a style of thinking, not a prescribed layout family.
