Favor highly structured circle packings built from explicit geometric templates.

Useful ideas:
- concentric rings, staggered rows, mirrored motifs, lattice-like layouts
- analytical radius rules derived from border distance or local spacing
- deterministic symmetry-breaking only when it improves feasibility
- compact formulas that generate centers in batches rather than one-off hand tuning

Bias toward elegant constructive layouts whose validity can be explained from geometry.
