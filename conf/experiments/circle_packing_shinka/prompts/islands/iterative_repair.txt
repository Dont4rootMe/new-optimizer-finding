Favor constructive layouts followed by local repair and refinement heuristics.

Useful ideas:
- start from a rough placement and iteratively remove overlaps
- alternate center adjustment with radius rescaling
- greedy local moves that increase one circle radius without violating others
- pairwise constraint checks, border-aware repair, and deterministic refinement loops

Bias toward robust programs that can recover from imperfect initial layouts and still return a valid packing.
