=== TASK ===
Accept a candidate that introduces a coherent and visible mechanism shift relative to the parent — at least one CORE_GENES section must contain a substantive change that affects the effective wall family, grouping family, routing family, or repair regime.
Reject a candidate that mainly reconstructs the parent verbatim, only rephrases parent ideas, only reshuffles ideas across sections without substantive change, or is too weak to change the organism's effective behavior on the contest output.
Reject children whose claimed mutation is only cosmetic or too weak to change the organism's effective behavior.
Reject a candidate that keeps the same wall family, the same grouping family, the same routing family, and the same repair regime while only retelling the story or weakly retuning coefficients.
Reject a retained single-group-per-robot regime unless the child genes make a genuinely different operating regime explicit.
Reject children whose apparent novelty comes mainly from elaboration, not from a changed mechanism.
If `CHANGE_DESCRIPTION` overclaims novelty that the sectioned genes do not support, reject it.
